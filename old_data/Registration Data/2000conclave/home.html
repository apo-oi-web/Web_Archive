Flashback to Conclave - October 22-24 - WPI (Worcester, MA)

Hey there guys & gals!

Come on over to Worcester Polytechnic Institute in Worcester, MA for Omicron Iota's fifties-themed Fall Conclave.

On Friday night, make your way to the WPI Campus Center for registration and some retro Friday night fun! Please bring unopened toiletry items (travel size is preferred) for our local shelters - the chapter bringing the most toiletries by Saturday afternoon wins a wonderful prize!

Saturday is chock full o' workshops, service, and APO goodness- all the tools you need to keep your chapters running smoothly! After an afternoon break, get all dolled up for the banquet and dance hosted on campus.

Since this conclave is the last big gathering before the National Convention in Atlanta (Dec 27-30), there's a bonus Sunday morning talk for chapter delegates, and any interested people, to discuss any pending legislation.
