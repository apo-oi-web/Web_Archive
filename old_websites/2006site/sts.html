Service To Students Nomination

The annual Service To Students award is given to a member of the faculty or staff who goes beyond their job to help students. Each year Alpha Phi Omega takes nominations from students to find a list of candidates. Each candidate is interviewed by members of our award committee who selects the recipient. A plaque is kept in Salisbury Labs to commemorate past winners.
