Omicron Iota History...

  • Omicron Iota was founded on December 18, 1964. Our chapter has been active since the founding.
  • Our first President was Philip Nyeberg
  • The first advisor of Omicron Iota was Professor Harlod Corey
  • Our big brother Chapter was Epsilon Delta (CCSU)
  • We first allowed women to pledge our chapter in 1972, four years prior to the National Faternity
  • We last held a Conclave in the Spring of 2000. Prior to that we held one in 1995
  • We are in Region 1 and Section 96
  • As our chapter considers the Distinguished Service Key to be a great honor, we have our honored two of our alumni this award. One of these awards was presented to John Grossi.
