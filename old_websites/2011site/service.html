The Omicron Iota chapter of Alpha Phi Omega is committed to performing useful community service in and around the Worcester community. We can do large projects as well as projects needing only a few volunteers, and can usually arrange our own transportation. If you have a community service opportunity and are looking for volunteers please contact our Service Vice President at apo-service@wpi.edu.
Groups we have worked with in the past include:

  • South Middlesex Opportunity Council (formerly PIP shelter)
  • Sherry's Kids/Why Me
  • Dismiss House
  • Worcester Food Bank
  • Worcester Veterans' Shelter
  • Special Olympics Massachusetts
  • Boys and Girls Club
  • Habitat for Humanity
  • Relay for Life
  • Camp Sunshine
  • Heiffer International - Overlook Farm
  • Tower Hill Botanical Garden
  • Audobon Society
  • Wachusett Greenways
  • Community Harvest Project
  • Equine Rescue
  • Friends of Institute Park
  • Mustard Seed Soup Kitchen
