Welcome!

Welcome to Alpha Phi Omega's Omicron Iota Chapter website. Alpha Phi Omega is Worcester Polytechnic Institute's one and only co-ed service fraternity. Our fraternity has served humanity since 1925, and our chapter has since 1964. Our chapter is dedicated to providing service to our fraternity members, our school, our community, and our nation. To learn more about us, see our about page. If you are interested in becoming part of our brotherhood, see the recruitment page. If you have any questions, you can contact us here.
